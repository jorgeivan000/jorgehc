About me

Hi there!, my name is Jorge Iván Herrera-Cámara and I am a software engineer. Born and raised in Mexico, I grew up to really enjoy working in teams and being surrounded by computers. I studied software engineering as a bachelors in Mexico. Afterwards, I worked for some years as a software engineer in the private sector in Mexico. Until I decided it was time to get my MS in Computer Science; which I studied and graduated from Texas A&M University in 2017.

I am currently working at Yellowme. A software engineering consulting company located in Yucatan, Mexico. My main areas of expertise are mobile application development for iOS using Swift and Android using Java. I am continuously working to augment my knowledge in Computer Science and improve my programming skills to become a more efficient engineer and productive teammate. I’m always eager to hear from new opportunities that may arise in the future.
